Masters in Interior Design

Master's degrees in interior design are frequently used to prepare students for managerial positions in the design industry or postsecondary teaching positions. Master's-level interior design programmes require 30 to 36 credits of subject-specific coursework, depending on the school, though some programmes require 45 credits. Master's degrees in Interior Design or a related programme title, such as interior architecture, sustainable interior design environments, or healthcare interior design, typically result in a Master of Arts (MA), Master of Science (MS), Master of Professional Studies (MPS), or Master of Fine Arts (MFA). A Master in Interior Design Course is a Postgraduate level degree course
