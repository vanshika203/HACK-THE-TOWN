ANIMATION and MULTIMEDIA

Animation and multimedia courses have grown in popularity among students in recent years, making them one of the most in-demand professional paths. Animation is the art of giving life to still images, texts etc. Animation is widely used in the modern days, in films, photographs and much more. Admission to these courses is based on merit or the marks secured in the qualifying exam or entrance exam (10 + 2). There are no specific entrance examinations other than the ones conducted by institutions and colleges independently.


UG Courses for Animation and Management

A Bachelor’s degree in Animation and multimedia can be obtained after 10+2 with a minimum score of 50% and a separate entrance examination (college specific). Degree courses provide extensive knowledge of the subject.

  • Bachelor of Design (BDes)
  • Bachelor of Science in Multimedia, Animation and Graphics Design
  • Bachelor of Computer Applications in Gaming and Graphics
  • Bachelor of Visual Arts in Game Development and Animation
  • Bachelor of Vocation in Graphics and Multimedia
  • Bachelor of Science in Multimedia
  • Bachelor of Animation and Multimedia
  • Bachelor of Aniamtion
  • Bachelor of Animation and Visual Effects
