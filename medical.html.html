PARAMEDICAL COURSES

The course duration is generally three years. Each academic year is usually divided into two semesters. This course structure may vary from one institute to another. Some of the notable degree paramedical courses are as follows –

  • Medical Laboratory Technology
  • Medical Imaging Technology
  • Blood Bank Technology
  • Dialysis Technology
  • OTT
  • X-Ray Technology
  • Radiography
  • Medical Record Technology
  • Ophthalmic Technology
  • Anaesthesia Technology
  • Audiology and Speech Therapy
