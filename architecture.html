ARCHITECTURE

Architecture courses extensively focus on the development of the skills which are essential for becoming an architect. Architectural courses consist of subjects and topics such as the process of construction of buildings, airport terminals, highway construction, malls, complexes and other civil construction by using various innovative ideas and other analytical skills.


Undergraduate Courses for Architecture Students

The Undergraduate courses are of duration three to four years. It involves the study of planning, designing, construction and management. National level and State level examinations like JEE, AIEEE, NATA and many more are conducted for the Architecture students for admission to UG programmes.

  • Bachelor of Architecture
  • B. E Construction Technology
  • Bachelor of Planning
  • Bachelor of Science in Residential Space Design and Management
  • Bachelor of Technology in Urban and Regional Planning

Diploma in Architecture Courses

Diploma Courses are the basic programs which deal with major subjects related to Architecture. After 12th students can either continue with Bachelor courses or Diploma courses. Architectural Diploma courses are three year duration programs through which students can study all the basics of major subjects of Architecture.

  • Diploma in Architectural Assistantship
  • Diploma in Architectural Engineering
  • Diploma in Construction Management
  • Diploma in Construction Technology
  • Foundation Diploma in Architecture and Design
