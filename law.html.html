Law

Law education is the most important need for the candidates who want to pursue and manage to complete the legal education and grab a prestigious post of Lawyer in society. Law degrees basically help us to understand the process of law and how it works. It is one of the most respectful and most sought-after course to study. Students pursue law courses at undergraduate, post-graduate, and doctorate levels to pursue a legal profession in India. People from all streams and academic backgrounds can pursue law after graduation.

Law Courses after 12th comprises great exposure to candidates who are pursuing successful Carriers in Law. Here is the list of some UG Law courses and their duration.

  • Bachelor of Law (LL.B)- 3 year
  • Bachelor of Legislative of Law (Honours)- 3 year
  • Bachelor of Law in Intellectual Property Rights – 3year
  • Bachelor of Business Administration & Bachelor of Legislative of Law (B.B.A.+ L.L.B)- 5 year

  • Bachelor of Arts & Bachelor of Legislative of Law (B.A. + LL.B)-5 year
