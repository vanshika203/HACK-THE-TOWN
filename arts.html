ARTS

Arts or Humanities is one of the popular streams which offer a plethora of courses. Arts / Humanities & Social Sciences comprise a wide variety of academic and professional disciplines, including English, Psychology, Political Science, History, Anthropology and Music. Arts graduates can pursue a wide variety of careers, including business, media, education, government, and non-profit work. Arts students can also explore their career in diversified such as journalism, fashion, aviation, law etc.

Diploma and PG Diploma in Arts Courses

Diploma or PG Diploma in Art courses are yet another good choice of study in terms of time effectiveness. Diploma or UG Diploma Art courses can be pursued directly after 10th or 12th from any stream (Arts, Science or Commerce) and the admissions in these courses are done based on merit scores in the previous qualification. A UG Diploma in Arts takes a time span of 2 years for completion whereas a PG Diploma in Arts takes 1 year for completion.

  • Diploma in Fine Arts
  • Diploma in Art and Craft
  • Diploma in Digital Photography
  • Diploma in Music
  • Diploma in Fashion Photography
  • Diploma in Performing Arts
  • PGD in Museology
  • PGD in Art and Design
  • PGD in Cinematography
  • PGD in TV Direction
