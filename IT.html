Information Technology

IT Courses or Information Technology Courses are one of the most studied subjects since the last decade. Information Technology (IT) is a rapidly growing sector, which focuses on the research of using computers and communications to be able to manage, gather, shop and move info. Both software and hardware companies are areas of Information Technology. Because of the significance and status of IT, the present age is usually known as the Information Technology age. One can pursue Information Technology at UG, PG and Doctorate levels. Given below are the popular IT courses offered by institutes and colleges across India.


UG Courses for IT Students

Choosing Information Technology as your undergraduate course right after 12th can help you achieve great heights with handsome salary pays offered by IT companies in the nearby future. From IITs to private colleges, almost all of them have UG courses for IT and allied branches because of its great scope in today's world as well as in tomorrow's approaching future.

Diploma and PG Diploma for IT Courses

Diploma or PG Diploma in IT courses are yet another good choice in terms of time effectiveness and for early job seeking purposes with handsome packages. Diploma or UG Diploma IT courses can be pursued directly after 10th or 12th and the admissions in these courses are done based on merit scores in the previous qualifications. A UG Diploma in IT takes a time span of 2 years for completion whereas a PG Diploma in IT generally takes a 1 year span for completion.
